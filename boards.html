Charcuterie Boards

Elegance Without the Effort

The Art of Sharing

There's something timeless about a beautiful charcuterie board: the colours, the textures, the pleasure of sharing good food with good company. At Farmhouse Artisan Cheese in Oakville, we turn that moment into an art form—without you having to lift a finger.

Our charcuterie boards are thoughtfully curated with premium cheeses, hand-selected cured meats, seasonal accompaniments, and artisan crackers. Every board showcases the same attention to quality and craft you'll find in our collection of over 120 specialty cheeses, ensuring each bite is balanced, flavourful, and memorable.

Your Way

You can enjoy our expertise in two easy ways:

Choose a beautifully arranged board crafted by Farmhouse Artisan Cheese, or bring your favourite board from home and let our team custom-fill it with the perfect mix of cheeses, charcuterie, and pairings tailored to your taste, guest list, and occasion.

(Minimum 2 business days required for advanced orders.)

Events

Planning a corporate event, client meeting, or team celebration? Our charcuterie spreads make hosting effortless and impressive. We also offer elegant seasonal gifting options, from curated boards to gift-ready selections, so you can wow clients, colleagues, friends, and family with zero stress.

Visit us in Oakville and chat with our knowledgeable staff about your next gathering. Whether you're hosting two people or twenty, we'll help you design a charcuterie experience that looks stunning, tastes incredible, and makes you the perfect host.
